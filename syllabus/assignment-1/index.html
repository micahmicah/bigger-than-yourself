Assignment 1

Reproduction & Form

The first assignment will be an examination of the human scale. Next week, bring to class a full size reproduction of yourself.

Take into consideration this moment of translation. Will your body be printed on a single piece of substrate? Projected? Will we see the pixels? Will we see the seams? Will there be color?

In addition to the reproduction of yourself, generate a piece of graphic form with the same method, at the same scale.

In addition to creating these reproductions. Travel to the ICA in Boston to view The Visitors by Ragnar Kjartansson. Document your experience and prepare for a discussion about the work. How it’s presented, how it’s framed.
