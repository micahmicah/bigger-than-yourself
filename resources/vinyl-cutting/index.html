Vinyl Cutting

Vinyl cutters in the area.

Concept Link: Now in the 2nd floor of the Design Center, Concept Link offers large format printing as well as vinyl cutting.

AS220 Labs: In addition to laser cutting, AS220 Labs also has a vinyl cutter which is available for use after attending a one day course. You may also hire the Labs staff to produce cut vinyl for you.
